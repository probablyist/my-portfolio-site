ES Stats

#Ruby on Rails #PostreSQL #CSS #Heroku #Git

When I trade futures I like to look for a statistical edge to help guide my trading descisions. I primarily trade the E-mini S&P 500 futures contract (a.k.a ES), and I like to know how frequently its price exceeds (breaches) certain price levels. This app takes and stores price history for the ES, notes price levels of interest, and calculates the frequency of price exceeding those levels.

Ideally I would have used an API to periodically pull the ES price history, but I was unable to find a suitable (i.e. free) data source that would provided me with the needed data. So instead, I'm using the slightly less automated method of copying and pasting price history from my trading platform into a Google Sheets spreadsheet which is published as a CSV file. Then I have Heroku Scheduler run the appropriate tasks in my Rails app to access and parse the CSV file, update the databases, and calculate the statistics.

The app was built using Rails 7, vanilla CSS, and PostreSQL. It is hosted on Heroku (I'm using the free tier dyno, so it may take ~30 seconds to spin up).
